CIS Student Learning Outcomes

The CIS graduate will be able to read and listen carefully to business and technology ideas and information, to understand, synthesize, and convey them to other people using both language and modes (written, oral, digital).

The CIS graduate will be able to identify, define, analyze, and respond to problems, challenges, and opportunities by employing logical, informed, and feasible solutions within the business and technology environment.

The CIS graduate will be able to learn (pursue knowledge) to work ethically, respectfully, and productively with people from diverse backgrounds operating in a multicultural environment while balancing personal and professional challenges.
